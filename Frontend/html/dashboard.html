Leave Policies

  • Sick Leave: 12 days annually - Medical certificate required for more than 2 consecutive days.
  • Casual Leave: 12 days annually - For personal reasons, advance notice required.
  • Annual Leave: 6 days annually - For vacation and personal time off.
  • Maternity Leave: 365 days (1 year) for female employees - Can be taken up to 3 times in career. Available only for female employees.
  • Leave applications must be submitted at least 3 days in advance (except emergency cases).
  • Unused leave cannot be carried over to the next year.
  • Emergency leave should be reported to HR as soon as possible.
